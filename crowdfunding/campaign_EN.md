## About TENGU

TENGU is a personal AI mentor that runs locally on your machine.
No subscriptions. No data collection. No corporate servers watching your thoughts.

While competitors charge $20/month, TENGU is free — today, tomorrow, and forever.

The name comes from Japanese mythology: the Tengu demon-teacher who trained samurai
in knowledge that schools refused to teach. That's exactly what we're building.
